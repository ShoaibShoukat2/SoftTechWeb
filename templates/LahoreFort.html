Exploring the Rich Heritage of Lahore Fort: A Journey Through Mughal Splendor.

Lahore Fort, located in Lahore, Pakistan, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its rich Mughal architecture. Built in the 16th century by Emperor Akbar, the fort houses beautiful palaces, gardens, and mosques. Its iconic Alamgiri Gate is a symbol of grandeur. The fort played a significant role in the region's history and stands as a testament to Mughal craftsmanship and design.
